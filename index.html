Rulebook

Round 1:

  • It will be a trivia round on web development-related topics.
  • A QR code will be displayed initially, redirecting participants to a website containing questions.
  • After completing the trivia round, a leaderboard will be displayed showcasing the points earned.

Round 2:

  • Teams will then participate to bid their favorable website domain for website building using the points earned in the previous round.
  • Following, a QR code will be displayed, redirecting to a GitHub repository containing a basic code template.
  • Teams will be challenged with commands that require them to implement specific features into their website (if completed, extra points will be given).
  • The Website building round will then continue for 45 minutes, after which the teams are required to submit their source file.
  • Teams will be judged on creativity, uniqueness and functionality of the website.
  • All participants must bring their laptops fully charged with a stable internet connection.
  • The Organizing Committee reserves the right to disqualify teams found using unfair means, with the decision being final.
